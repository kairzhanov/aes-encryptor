About AES Encryptor/Decryptor


AES Encryptor/Decryptor is an online tool that provides a simple way to protect sensitive data with a powerful encryption algorithm.

AES Encryptor/Decryptor uses AES which is considered one of the strongest algorithms available.

The encryption process is fast, and the website itself is lightweight and easy to use.

It's fast and free and open source!

AES Encryptor/Decryptor offers no tracking mechanisms. This helps to keep your privacy safe while browsing on any device or computer.


How to use AES Encryptor/Decryptor ?

Text encryption

Enter your AES key in the key box, Then change the key type as appropriate. Then click Encrypt. To decrypt data, The same process as the encryption.

File encryption

Enter your AES key in the key box, Then change the key type as appropriate Select your file, Then click Upload and Encrypt, The same process for the decryption.

Important !

If you want to read your own encrypted data, you'll need to have the same key that was used in the encryption.
So, when you lose or forget the encryption key for a file or text, it is impossible to recover it.
